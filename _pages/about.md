I am a Master's student at **Hefei University of Technology**.

My research interests lie in **computer vision**, especially:

- Source-Free Domain Adaptive Object Detection
- Domain Adaptation
- Vision Foundation Models
- Reliable Pseudo-Label Learning


## Research

My current research focuses on improving the reliability of self-training under domain shift. 
I study how external semantic knowledge from vision foundation models can provide complementary reliability estimation for source-free domain adaptation.
