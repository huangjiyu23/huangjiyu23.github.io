I am a Master's student in Artificial Intelligence at **Hefei University of Technology**.

My research interests focus on **computer vision**, especially:

- Source-Free Domain Adaptive Object Detection
- Domain Adaptation
- Vision Foundation Models
- Reliable Self-training


## Research

My current research focuses on improving the reliability of pseudo-label learning under domain shifts. 
I explore how vision foundation models can provide semantic reliability estimation for source-free domain adaptation.
